About the Project

Lakmaga is a gamified virtual reality educational platform developed to address the limitations of traditional STEM education in Sri Lanka. The system leverages immersive 3D environments and interactive simulations to transform abstract theoretical concepts into tangible learning experiences.

Set within a historically reconstructed Sigiriya village, the platform integrates mathematics, science, technology, and engineering concepts into culturally contextualized tasks. By combining gamification principles with situated learning theory, Lakmaga promotes active learning, critical thinking, and long-term knowledge retention.

Key Features

Pioneering a new standard for interactive classroom education.

Immersive 3D Environment

An immersive 3D learning environment closely based on authentic Sri Lankan heritage, such as the ancient Sigiriya rock fortress.

Curriculum-Aligned STEM

Curriculum-aligned interactive STEM modules mapped strictly in accordance with the national middle school syllabus.

Gamified Interactions

Interactive gamified tasks equipped with real-time feedback mechanisms that make academic challenges engaging.

Passive Telemetry

A passive telemetry system designed for objective performance evaluation without disrupting the student's VR experience.

How It Works

A seamless, intuitive educational pipeline.

1. Enter VR Environment

Equip the headset and step instantly into the ancient Sigiriya village reconstruction.

2. Interact with Tasks

Engage physically with the 3D ecosystem to solve STEM puzzles and challenges.

3. Receive Feedback

Get in-game real-time responses to guide learning and correct misconceptions instantly.

4. Performance tracking

Educators receive passive telemetry data tracking interaction success rates and struggles.
