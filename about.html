Project Background

The Lakmaga project is developed by final-year Information Technology undergraduate students at the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT). Our mission is to fundamentally improve STEM education in Sri Lanka by designing an immersive, curriculum-aligned VR technology platform that breaks the limitations of traditional classroom boundaries.

Key Achievements

Developed a comprehensive VR learning system mapped directly to local syllabuses.

Successfully implemented engaging gamification loops within STEM education modules.

Created a deeply culturally relevant internal environment (ancient Sigiriya).
